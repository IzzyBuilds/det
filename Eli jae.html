quareball Entertainment singer-songwriter Eli Jae returns with a magnetic new single, Your Body, the follow-up to his steamy track, Sexual Healing, and a standout from his upcoming debut album, Soundtrack to Great Sex. Produced by the highly acclaimed record producer LeriQ, the sultry, hypnotic R&B cut is a masterstroke in mood and sensuality.

Your Body is an ode to desire in its purest, most physical form. With a slow-burning tempo, velvety textures, and Eli’s emotionally expressive vocals, the track captures the electricity of unspoken connection. His delivery is fluid and confident, yet deeply vulnerable, as he croons, “Your body is calling my body… I like it, don’t stop, girl,” inviting listeners into a private moment where passion and energy speak louder than words.

The track pulses with warm basslines, ambient synths, and subtle Afrofusion elements—hallmarks of LeriQ’s production style—creating a lush sonic backdrop that feels tailor-made for late-night drives, dim lights, and close conversations. It’s a sound that perfectly complements Eli Jae’s lyrical intimacy and vocal finesse.

Your Body doesn’t just extend the narrative of Soundtrack to Great Sex—it deepens it. Where Sexual Healing leaned into emotional vulnerability, this follow-up dives confidently into physical chemistry, offering a different but equally compelling facet of modern love and connection. It’s a slow jam that respects the legacy of classic R&B while pushing the genre forward with Eli’s distinctive voice and global influences.

With each release, Eli Jae continues to position himself as a bold new voice in contemporary R&B—effortlessly merging soul, Afrofusion, and modern production into music that is emotionally resonant and undeniably sexy. Your Body is more than just a song, it’s a full-body experience.
